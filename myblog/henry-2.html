Henry

Henry used to sleep on his back and with his mouth wide open.
Henry used to have brown eyes so black they’d suck in air.
Henry used to have hair on his lip that would scratch me.
Henry used to go to the same college as me.
Henry used to study English and Biology.
Henry used to say we’d work in the park together.
Henry used to bartend in Seattle and visit on weekends.
Henry used to call his mom when I was over so I could say hi.
Henry used to own a car that only fit the two of us.
Henry used to sneak into the park to bring me PBRs and new CDs.
Henry used to play bass guitar for a tiny band in Port Angeles.
Henry used to write songs for me.
Henry used to bring a bag of daisies to feed the hares.
Henry used to be scared of goats.
Henry used to hug the trees and get moss on his jacket.
Henry used to love Andrei Tarkovsky and Stalker and pretentious movies.
Henry used to sneak into the south tower so I didn’t have to sleep alone.
Henry used to smoke cigarettes on the ledge and store the ash in his pocket so he didn’t litter.
Henry used to be mindful like that.
Henry used to have a tiny crease on his cheek from where he got stitches in second grade.
Henry used to have a tilted nose from when he broke it in high school.
Henry used to just say words.
Henry used to breathe slowly to moderate his breath.
Henry used to have lungs and a heart and two kidneys and a liver and a pancreas.
Henry used to have hair on his head and hair on chest and hair on his ass and hair on his lips.

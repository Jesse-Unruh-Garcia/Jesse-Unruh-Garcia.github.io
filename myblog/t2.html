South Tower

The South Tower (known as T2 on the radio) is the ranger tower located on the south end of the park. It’s located at the second highest point of the park, and is a bit more of a cabin than anything else. The South Tower is cozier and more comfortable and warmer than the North Tower, since it was made newer and isn’t crumbling. During my first couple rounds in the park, they exclusively put me in the South Tower. Henry would go on “camping” trips from work where he would sneak into T2 and stay with me for a couple of days. Usually brought s'mores or cds or games along with him. Little reminders to keep a part of him in the tower.

My eye twitches when I have to look at the tower now. Like my eyes don’t want to stare at it for too long.

The new trainee, Rachel, is in there now. She usually finds old stuff of mine there: beanies, walking sticks, copies of Silent Spring. Have never spoken to her but she likes to mess with me. The south tower doesn’t have a radio like the north tower does, but it has a massive flashlight to signal to me. She’s started sending me recommendations in morse code. B for books, M for music, F for films, G for games.

Last week she sent: ..-. / .--. .-. .. -- . .-.

F Primer.

Nerd!

But it’s nice, knowing someone cares to talk even without eyes or mouths or words.

What Have I Done?

I have worked at Olympics for three years.
I have been a friend to Doc Clark.
I have been a “ray of hope for the youth.”
I have witnessed the wild turn of an abstract.
I have recorded its changes.
I have been changed by it.
I was changed before it.

I have lived with the trees and the moss and the hares.
I have lived with bubbling life and the unexpected.
I have lived with the incredible and the strange.
I have lived for three years in a geometric spiral with my dual sun.
I have spent the past month spinning alone.
I expected him to supernova.
I have realized most go like a flicker.

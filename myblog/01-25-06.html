January 25th, 2006: Hi Mom! Hi Dad!

Mom and Dad say they’ve started reading my nature blog. I was wondering why the traffic said there were two viewers instead of one. And they even said they’re going to share it with grandma and grandpa!

The tower has a computer that has two real functions: email and music. Email is used for talking to Doc Clark, other rangers, the researchers in Port Angeles, and friends. The music is on there because I was able to download Limewire before they put any restrictions. If I’m lucky, I can download a four-five minute song every couple of days! This is one of about three places in the entire park that gets any sort of internet access, mostly through a jumble of wires and tech mumbo jumbo, but that doesn’t mean it's good. There’s a reason most of my access is text only.

Doc Clark says I need to hang back on the swearing, even though this is my website. Yes Doctor, I’m sorry for saying “fuck” in my post yesterday. Yes Doctor, I won’t say “fuck” again.

One of the goats kicked me in the freaking shin this morning. Caught me by surprise. Chased it down for about ten minutes before I gave up. They’re cute and all but I can’t wait till they get them all out of here. They’re invasive, not supposed to be in the Olympic Peninsula at all. Almost makes you feel bad, since they don’t even know that they’re not supposed to be here. Little guys just kick around because it’s all they know.

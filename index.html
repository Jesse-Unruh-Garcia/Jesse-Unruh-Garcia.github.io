Hello World!
I write this now from the outside looking in. What started as an experiment, an expression, a form of therapy designed to recreate me, was usurped by something greater than it all. When I heard the news about Henry for the first time, I thought that was the strangest life could be. How very unclear it seems to me now, knowing what I know about death and the severity of it. But also of creating, and its suddenness.

I’ve tried my best, I realize now. And all I have left are the artifacts for people to power through. Let’s hope I formatted them to the best of my abilities.

Truth is, I can't help but hide parts of myself on this website. And so, I've chosen to make a game of it. Find a part of me and I'll find a part of you.

Certain words, those ones that mean the most to me, will beat like my heart. Flickering, popping in and out of the page.
